About the Barangay

Guided by Purpose,
Driven by Community

Barangay Santa Catalina is committed to upholding the rights and welfare of every resident through accessible, efficient, and transparent governance.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide accessible, efficient, and transparent government services to every resident of Barangay Santa Catalina. We are committed to maintaining peace, order, and sustainable community development through inclusive governance and active citizen participation.

🌟

Our Vision

A progressive, peaceful, and prosperous Barangay Santa Catalina where every resident enjoys a high quality of life, supported by a responsive local government that upholds justice, unity, and community empowerment.

System Features

Everything You Need
in One Portal

BIMS brings key barangay services online — saving you time and making governance more transparent for everyone.

📝

Online Blotter Filing

File incident reports online at any time, from anywhere. No need to visit the barangay hall to report an incident.

🔍

Blotter Case Tracking

Monitor the real-time status of your filed blotter using your reference number. Stay informed every step of the way.

👥

Frontdesk Management

Barangay staff can manage, update, and process all filed blotters and resident requests through a dedicated dashboard.

📊

Admin Dashboard

Full administrative control over the system — manage officials, generate reports, and monitor barangay operations.

🔔

Notifications & Alerts

Get timely updates on your case status and important barangay announcements directly through the system.

🔒

Secure & Private

All resident data and case information are securely stored and protected. Your privacy is our top priority.

How It Works

File a Blotter in
4 Simple Steps

Our streamlined process makes reporting incidents quick and straightforward for all residents.

1

Visit the Portal

Access the BIMS portal from any device — mobile, tablet, or desktop.

2

Fill the Form

Complete the blotter form with accurate details about the incident.

3

Submit & Receive ID

Submit your report and receive a unique reference number to track your case.

4

Track Your Case

Use your reference number to monitor your case status anytime, anywhere.
