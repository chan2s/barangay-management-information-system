About the Barangay

Guided by Purpose,
Driven by Community

Barangay Poblacion is committed to upholding the rights and welfare of every resident through accessible, efficient, and transparent governance.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, efficient, and transparent government services to every resident of Barangay Poblacion. We are committed to maintaining peace, order, and sustainable community development through inclusive governance and active citizen participation.

Our Vision

A progressive, peaceful, and prosperous Barangay Poblacion where every resident enjoys a high quality of life, supported by a responsive local government that upholds justice, unity, and community empowerment.

System Features

Everything You Need
in One Portal

BIMS brings key barangay services online — saving you time and making governance more transparent for everyone.

Online Blotter Filing

File incident reports online at any time, from anywhere. No need to visit the barangay hall to report an incident.

Certificate Requests

Request Barangay Clearance, Certificate of Indigency, Residency Certificate, and Barangay ID online.

Certificate Tracking

Track the status of your certificate requests in real-time. Get updates on processing and availability.

Blotter Case Tracking

Monitor the real-time status of your filed blotter using your reference number. Stay informed every step of the way.

Admin Dashboard

Full administrative control over the system — manage officials, generate reports, and monitor barangay operations.
